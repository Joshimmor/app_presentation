Normalization (2NF)

Eliminated partial dependencies. Insurance table separated to adhere to 3NF principles and reduce data redundancy.

Many-to-Many Logic

CaregiverCertification acts as a joint table, resolving the M:M relationship between Caregivers and their various Certifications.

Geospatial Data

Appointment stores lat/long to enable real-time distance calculation and caregiver matching.

Contextual Chat

Chat is strictly linked to Appointment, ensuring communication history is tied to specific care sessions.
